Blackberry Clafoutis

Clafoutis is a French dessert custard dish. I first made this dish when I had picked a lot of blackberries one season. If, like me, you enjoy a custard-type
dish, then you should enjoy this dish. I would also liken it somewhat to the texture of a thin pancake.
